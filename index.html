Science

Science is the systematic study of the natural and physical world through observation and experimentation.

Branches of Science

Science is further divided into three main branches, and they are:

  • Physics
    • Mechanics
    • Biophysics
    • Geophysics
    • Atomic Physics
    • Nuclear Physics
    • Modern Physics
    • Classical Physics
    • Astrophysics
    • Thermodynamics
    • Optics
    • Acoustics
  • Chemistry
    • Physical Chemistry
    • Analytical Chemistry
    • Materials Chemistry
    • Biochemistry
    • Organic Chemistry
    • Inorganic Chemistry
    • Theoretical Chemistry
  • Biology
    • Anatomy
    • Genetics
    • Microbiology
    • Physiology
    • Taxonomy

Physics

Physics is the branch of science that deals with the physical phenomenon related to the objects around us.

Mechanics:
The branch of physics that deals with the action of forces on bodies and with motion.
